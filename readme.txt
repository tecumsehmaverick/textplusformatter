Formatter: Text Plus
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Version: 1.0.1
Author: Rowan Lewis <rowan@pixelcarnage.com>
Build Date: 15 May 2009
Requirements: Symphony 2


Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Upload the 'textplusformatter' folder in this archive to your Symphony
	'extensions' folder.

2.  Enable it by selecting the "Formatter: Text Plus", choose Enable from the
	with-selected menu, then click Apply.

4.	You can now choose "Text Plus" as a text formatter.
